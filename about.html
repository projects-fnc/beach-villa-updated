About Our Villa

Discover the story behind Kerala's most exclusive beachfront retreat

Introduction to Vadanappilly Beach Villa

Nestled along the pristine shores of Vadanappilly, our beach villa represents the culmination of a dream to create Kerala's most exclusive coastal retreat. Established with a vision to blend traditional Keralite hospitality with modern luxury, we offer an intimate escape that feels both authentic and indulgent.

Our journey began with a simple philosophy: to create a space where guests could experience the true essence of coastal Kerala while enjoying world-class amenities and personalized service.
